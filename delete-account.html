FightGrid Account Deletion Instructions Overview FightGrid allows all users to permanently delete their account and all associated data. This page explains how to request deletion, what data is removed, what data may be temporarily retained, and how long retention lasts. How to Delete Your FightGrid Account You can delete your account in two ways: 1. Delete Your Account Inside the App Open the FightGrid app Go to Settings → Account Select Delete Account Confirm the deletion request Your account will be permanently deleted. 2. Request Deletion by Email If you cannot access the app, you may request deletion by email. Send a message to: onlydavis34@gmail.com Subject: Account Deletion Request Include the email or phone number associated with your FightGrid account. Data That Will Be Deleted When your account is deleted, FightGrid permanently removes: Account profile Name, email, phone number Fighter profile details Fight records Scorecards Photos and videos Messages Event participation history Identity verification documents Analytics data associated with your account Device identifiers linked to your account Any cloud‑stored FightGrid data associated with your profile All deleted data is removed from active systems. Data Temporarily Retained For security, fraud prevention, and legal compliance, FightGrid may retain the following for up to 30 days: Backup copies stored in secure encrypted archives Server logs containing your user ID Transaction records related to purchases (required by Google Play) These backups are automatically purged and cannot be used to restore your account. Data That Cannot Be Deleted Immediately Some data may remain for legal or financial compliance reasons, including: Google Play Billing transaction records Fraud prevention logs Security audit logs These records are not used for personalization or app functionality.
